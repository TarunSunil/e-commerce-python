ğŸ§  RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM DETAILS
================================================================================

CONTENT-BASED FILTERING ALGORITHM
----------------------------------
- Similarity Metric: Jaccard Index
- Feature Used: Product categories (tags)
- Formula:
  Jaccard(A, B) = |A âˆ© B| / |A âˆª B|
  Where A and B are sets of categories

EXAMPLE:
--------
Product A: ["electronics", "smartphone", "5G"]
Product B: ["electronics", "tablet", "5G"]

Intersection: {"electronics", "5G"}  â†’ 2 items
Union: {"electronics", "smartphone", "5G", "tablet"}  â†’ 4 items

Similarity = 2/4 = 0.5 (50% similar)

RECOMMENDATION STRATEGIES
-------------------------
1. Product-Based: Find N most similar products by category overlap
2. User-Based: Match products to user preferences (stored in user.preferences)
3. Popularity Fallback: Sort by stock level if no preferences exist
